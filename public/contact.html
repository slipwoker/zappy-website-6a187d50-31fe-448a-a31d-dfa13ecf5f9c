Quick answers

Find the information you need about sizing, styling, and our commitment to serving busy corporate professionals.

How do I choose the right fit for office days?

Start by taking your measurements following our detailed size guide. We recommend measuring your bust, waist, and hips while wearing the undergarments you'll pair with our pieces. If you're between sizes, consider your preference: size down for a tailored, structured fit or size up for more ease and comfort during long workdays. Our customer service team is always available to provide personalized fit recommendations based on your measurements and style preferences.

Are pieces designed for corporate dress codes?

Absolutely. Every piece in our collection is designed with professional-first silhouettes that meet traditional corporate dress codes while maintaining fashion-forward style. From structured blazers to elegant sheath dresses, our garments are crafted to empower you in boardrooms, client meetings, and executive presentations. We understand the importance of looking polished and confident in professional settings.

What if I'm between sizes?

Being between sizes is common, and we're here to help you decide. Consider the garment type: for structured pieces like blazers and tailored pants, sizing up often provides better comfort and movement. For dresses and tops with stretch fabric, your usual size typically works well. Check the product description for fabric composition and stretch information. When in doubt, reach out to our team with your measurements, and we'll guide you to the perfect fit based on the specific piece you're considering.

Do you offer styling guidance for specific roles?

Yes! We love helping our clients build wardrobes that align with their professional roles and personal style. Whether you're in finance, law, tech, or creative industries, we can provide tailored recommendations. Send us a message describing your role, typical work environment, and any specific dress code requirements. We'll suggest pieces that work seamlessly with your professional context while celebrating your individual style and body confidence.

How soon will I hear back?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours, Monday through Friday. For urgent styling questions or fit concerns, we prioritize quick responses during business hours (9 AM – 6 PM EST). If you reach out over the weekend, expect to hear from us first thing Monday morning. We're committed to providing thoughtful, personalized service to every client who trusts us with their professional wardrobe.
